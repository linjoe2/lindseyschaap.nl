Computer Science teacher

high school / Kesper college / 2021

As the computer science teacher at Kesper College, I was faced with a challenging situation. The students had gone without a teacher for two years, and with exams looming just two months away, they were in desperate need of guidance and support.

To achieve this, I used a range of innovative teaching techniques, including bringing in a broken computer for the students to disassemble and other interactive classes. By making the classes fun and interactive, I was able to engage the students and help them to understand complex concepts in a way that was easy to grasp.

The results were truly impressive. Thanks to my efforts, all of the students were able to pass their exams, despite the challenging circumstances. I am proud to have played a role in helping these students achieve their goals and to have inspired a passion for computer science that will stay with them for years to come.
